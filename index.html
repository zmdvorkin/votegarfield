What We Stand For

Our Platform

01

Student Voice & Representation

Every student deserves to be heard. We will establish clear, accessible channels for all Bulldogs to raise concerns, propose ideas, and hold ASB accountable — ensuring no voice goes unheard.

02

Transparency & Accountability

ASB should be an open book. We commit to publishing meeting notes, decisions, and budgets so the whole school community knows exactly what their student government is doing on their behalf.

03

School Spirit & Community

Garfield has an incredible community. We will elevate events, celebrate our diversity, and build traditions that bring every class, club, and corner of GHS together as one school.

04

Professional Leadership

We believe ASB deserves to be taken seriously. We bring a new standard of professionalism — showing up prepared, communicating clearly, and treating every student's concern with the respect it deserves.

05

Better School Resources

We will advocate directly to administration for improved facilities, student spaces, and the resources needed to make Garfield a school where everyone can thrive and succeed.

06

A Platform That Delivers

Campaigns make promises. We make commitments — and we'll check in publicly on our progress throughout the year so you can hold us to exactly what we said we'd do.
